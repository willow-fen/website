What is Forest School?

Forest School is a child-centred learning approach taking place in the woods, allowing children to play, explore and learn at their own pace and in their own ways. This is supported by trained practitioners to ensure the safety and wellbeing of those who attend.

During sessions, you are likely to find children climbing trees, creating concoctions in the mud kitchen, using a variety of tools, and learning how to make and cook on a fire.
